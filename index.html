-- RULES --

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times
    as he/she wishes.🎲 Each result gets added to his CURRENT score.
  • But, if the player rolls a 1, all his/her ROUND score gets lost.
    After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The player can choose to 'HOLD' ⏹, which means that his CURRENT
    score gets added to his MAIN TOTAL After that, it's the next
    player's turn again.
  • The first player to reach 100 points on MAIN TOTAL, wins
    the game. 🥳
  • Winner side will be highlighted with 🟢 color.🤩
    And the looser side will be highlighted with ⚫ color.😙
  • You can start a new game by clicking on 'New Game' button. 🔄
